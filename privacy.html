Privacy Policy

How Whispr handles privacy for anonymous voice conversations.

1. Introduction

Whispr is a premium anonymous voice-chat app. This Privacy Policy explains how Whispr collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app or contact us at support@getwhisprs.com.

2. Anonymous Use

Whispr is designed without profiles, photos, followers, or direct messaging. We use anonymous Firebase Authentication so the app can function without requiring a public identity, profile page, or social graph.

3. Information We Collect

We may collect technical identifiers created through anonymous Firebase Authentication, device and app information, usage events needed to operate the service, reports, blocks, and support messages you send to us.

4. Voice Conversations

Whispr uses Agora voice technology to enable live 90-second voice conversations. Whispr does not provide profiles, photos, or direct messaging between users. We do not sell personal data.

5. Microphone Permission

Whispr asks for microphone permission so you can participate in live voice conversations. You can disable microphone access in your device settings, but the voice-chat feature will not work without it.

6. Firebase and Firestore

Whispr uses Firebase services, including Firebase Authentication and Firestore, to support anonymous access, app state, safety controls, reports, blocks, and service reliability.

7. Reports and Blocks

When you report or block another user, we may store information related to that safety action so we can review abuse, prevent repeat contact, enforce our rules, and protect the service.

8. How We Use Information

We use information to operate Whispr, connect anonymous voice conversations, maintain safety tools, respond to support requests, improve reliability, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

9. When We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Whispr, including Firebase and Agora. We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect users, or to investigate misuse of the service.

10. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably needed for the purposes described in this Policy, including app operation, safety review, abuse prevention, legal compliance, and support.

11. Your Choices and Rights

You may contact us to request access, deletion, or other privacy assistance. Because Whispr uses anonymous authentication, we may need information from your device or app session to locate data associated with your use.

12. Children

Whispr is not intended for children under 18. People under 18 should not use the app.

13. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No online service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

14. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as Whispr changes. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and provide notice where appropriate.

15. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact support@getwhisprs.com.
